Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

KG ProDesign ("we", "us", "our") operates The Imposter Game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party payment providers: Stripe, PayPal, Cash App)
  • Game settings and preferences stored locally on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics (game sessions, feature usage)
  • IP address and general location data

2.3 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Premium session tokens
  • User preferences and settings
  • Theme selections

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process premium feature payments
  • Improve user experience and game features
  • Monitor service usage and detect technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Payment Processing

We do not store or process credit card information directly. All payment transactions are handled by:

  • Stripe - PCI DSS compliant payment processor
  • PayPal - Industry-standard payment platform
  • Cash App - Secure peer-to-peer payment service

Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for details on how they handle your payment information.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Maintain your session and premium status
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns

We may use Google AdSense for advertising, which may use cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

  • Premium session data: Stored locally for 24 hours after purchase
  • Game statistics: Stored locally on your device until cleared
  • Usage analytics: Retained for 90 days
  • Payment records: Maintained by payment processors per their policies

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure client-side data obfuscation
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No storage of sensitive payment information

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • PayPal - Payment processing
  • Cash App - Payment processing
  • Google AdSense - Advertising (free tier only)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data via browser developer tools
  • Delete your local data by clearing browser storage
  • Opt out of personalized ads through browser settings
  • Request information about data we may have collected

10. Children's Privacy

The Imposter Game is designed for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. International Users

The Service is operated in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

14. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact KG ProDesign through the game interface.
